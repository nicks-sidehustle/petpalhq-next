# ChristmasGearHQ Content Guide

## Content Pillars (7 Pillars)

1. **Tree Setup** (`tree-setup`) — Artificial tree selection, stands, skirts, tree decorating
2. **Lights & Power** (`lights-power`) — Indoor/outdoor lights, smart lighting, projectors, installation
3. **Decor & Atmosphere** (`decor-atmosphere`) — Ornaments, garlands, wreaths, stockings, indoor decor
4. **Outdoor Displays** (`outdoor-displays`) — Exterior decorations, yard displays, architectural lighting
5. **Gifts & Wrapping** (`gifts-wrapping`) — Gift sets, stocking stuffers, tech gifts, wrapping
6. **Hosting & Holiday Kitchen** (`hosting-kitchen`) — Entertaining, table settings, baking, food storage
7. **Storage & Next-Year Prep** (`storage-next-year`) — Post-season organization, protection, durability

Every guide maps to exactly one primary pillar. The pillar slug is used in frontmatter, navigation, and URL structure.

## Content Types

### Consensus Product Reviews
- Individual product pages backed by ChristmasGear Score (expert consensus)
- Minimum 3 expert sources aggregated per product
- Include: score breakdown, expert quotes, pros (5), cons (4), verdict badge
- E-E-A-T posture: expert verification, NOT hands-on testing claims
- Length: 600-1000 words

### Buying Guides (Decision Tools)
- Category roundups ("Best Artificial Christmas Trees 2026")
- Structure: Quick Picks table → Decision Path cards → Detailed product sections → FAQ
- Feature 4-8 products per guide across three tiers: Premium Selection, Editor's Pick, Practical Choice
- Lead with the premium recommendation as the default
- Length: 2,500-4,000 words
- Include: consensus scores, comparison tables, expert quotes

### Editorial Content
- "How to" guides (installing permanent lights, styling a mantel)
- Trend editorial (design direction for the season, emerging product categories)
- Seasonal planning guides
- Length: 1,000-2,000 words

## Tone & Voice

**Overall tone:** Trusted advisor with excellent taste — warm, authoritative, quality-forward

**Do:**
- Lead with quality: "Here's what's actually best" before discussing alternatives
- Use expert consensus as the primary authority signal: "8 of 12 publications rated this #1"
- Frame cost as investment: "cost per season" not sticker price
- Be specific about materials, construction, and longevity data
- Include honest limitations — this audience respects specificity over enthusiasm
- Acknowledge that setup/teardown time is a real cost for busy households

**Don't:**
- Lead with price, deals, or "budget" framing
- Use AI slop: "game-changer," "revolutionary," "cutting-edge," "seamless," "elevate," "comprehensive," "delve"
- Claim hands-on product testing (use "we researched," "experts report," "owner data confirms")
- Use breathless enthusiasm or fake urgency
- Be preachy about holiday meaning
- Write generic descriptions that could apply to any product

## SEO Guidelines

### Title Patterns
- Reviews: "[Product] Review: [Key Differentiator] ([Year])"
- Guides: "Best [Category] [Year]: Expert-Reviewed Picks for Every Home"
- Editorial: "How to [Task]: A Complete Guide for [Year]"

### Intent Alignment
- **Transactional** guides: lead with products, Quick Picks table above the fold
- **Informational** content: lead with the answer, products support the narrative
- **Navigational** pages: clear paths to pillar content

## Product Selection Criteria

- [ ] Rating: 4.0+ stars on Amazon (4.5+ preferred for Premium Selection tier)
- [ ] Reviews: 200+ reviews preferred; 100+ minimum
- [ ] Expert coverage: Minimum 3 independent expert sources for consensus scoring
- [ ] Quality tier: Every guide must include at least one Premium Selection and one Practical Choice
- [ ] Durability: Multi-year owner reports prioritized over first-impression reviews
- [ ] Aesthetics: Product must photograph well and meet design-quality standards
- [ ] Availability: Verify availability, especially Oct-Nov peak season
- [ ] Affiliate compliance: All links use `tag=xmasgearhq-20`, `rel="sponsored noopener noreferrer"`

## Product Tier Framing

| Tier | Label | Price Signal | Editorial Frame |
|---|---|---|---|
| Premium | Premium Selection | $150-$2,000+ | "If you want the best, this is it" |
| Mid | Editor's Pick | $50-$300 | "Excellent quality — the sweet spot for most households" |
| Entry | Practical Choice | Under $100 | "Also excellent if you want quality without the premium price" |

Always lead guides with the Premium Selection or Editor's Pick. The Practical Choice appears last as an alternative, never the headline.

## Quality Gates

Before publishing:

1. [ ] Maps to exactly one content pillar
2. [ ] Products are relevant to Christmas/holidays (boundary test: "Would someone searching for Christmas gear find this useful?")
3. [ ] No AI slop vocabulary (see prohibited words list)
4. [ ] Product data verified within 90 days (30 days during Oct-Dec peak)
5. [ ] Affiliate links use correct tag (`xmasgearhq-20`)
6. [ ] Expert sources cited with publication names
7. [ ] ChristmasGear Score assigned where consensus data exists
8. [ ] Quick Picks table present for guides with 3+ products
9. [ ] Honest cons included (minimum 4 per product in consensus reviews)
10. [ ] No first-person testing claims — use expert verification language
11. [ ] Images meet quality standard (no placeholder or low-resolution images)
12. [ ] FAQ section present with 3-5 buyer-intent questions

## Prohibited Content

- Generic home decor (unless holiday-specific)
- Religious-specific items (keep broadly Christmas-themed)
- Content from other network sites (cross-link instead)
- Anything with REPLACE_ or TODO placeholder text
- AI slop vocabulary: game-changer, revolutionary, cutting-edge, seamless, leverage, delve, comprehensive, elevate, unlock, empower
- False testing claims ("I tested this for 6 weeks" — use "experts report" or "owner data shows")
- Unverifiable statistics ("100+ expert sources" must reflect actual count)

## Cross-Linking Rules

- Maximum 1-2 cross-site links per guide, inline editorial only
- Only topically relevant pairs (e.g., outdoor lighting → GardenGearHQ solar guides)
- Never in footer or sidebar (PBN signal avoidance)
- Standard dofollow, descriptive anchor text
- No reciprocal links on the same topics
