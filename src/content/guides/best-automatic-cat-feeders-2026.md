## What to Look for in an Automatic Cat Feeder

**Dispensing accuracy:** This is the most important functional consideration. Feeders that over- or under-dispense by more than 10% of the target portion can contribute to weight issues over time. The PetSafe Smart Feed and PETLIBRO have the best real-world dispensing accuracy in this category.

**Jam resistance:** Auger-based feeders (used by most feeders under $100) are prone to jamming with small, round, or irregularly shaped kibble. If your cat eats small-breed kibble, look for feeders with conveyor belt mechanisms or test with a full hopper before committing.

**Power backup:** Cats fed on a schedule can become stressed or destructive when meals are missed. Any feeder you use regularly should have battery backup for power outages.

**Ease of cleaning:** Food residue in hoppers and chutes can harbor bacteria and mold. Look for hoppers and food contact surfaces that are dishwasher safe or at least easy to disassemble and hand wash.

**Smart home integration:** If you're already invested in a smart home ecosystem, look for feeders that work with Alexa or Google Home — you can tie feeding schedules into your existing routines. ## FAQ

### Are automatic feeders safe for cats?

Yes, with proper setup. The main risks are mechanical jams causing missed meals and portion inaccuracy causing over or underfeeding. Mitigate these by testing dispense accuracy before relying on the feeder, cleaning the unit monthly, and having a backup plan (a neighbor, pet sitter) for extended absences.

### Can I use an automatic feeder for wet food?

Most feeders are designed for dry kibble only. Wet food can jam standard dispensing mechanisms and will spoil quickly in a warm hopper. The Cat Mate C500 and SureFeed Microchip are the options designed to handle wet or raw food safely.

### How many meals per day should I set?

Most veterinary nutritionists recommend 2 meals per day for adult cats on a measured-calorie diet, or 3 meals for kittens. Spreading meals across the day (3-4 smaller portions) can help cats that vomit after eating too quickly.

### Will an automatic feeder work when the power goes out?

Only if it has battery backup. Always check the spec sheet. The PetSafe Smart Feed, PETLIBRO, and WOPET all include battery backup. The Cat Mate C500 runs on AA batteries only — no AC adapter — which makes it inherently outage-resistant.

### How do I stop my cat from figuring out how to open the feeder?

Curious cats (especially Bengals and Maine Coons) sometimes learn to paw food out of the dispensing chute or tip feeders over. Look for feeders with weighted non-slip bases and dispensing openings too small for a paw to fit inside. The PetSafe Smart Feed's dispensing chute geometry is specifically designed to prevent this.
