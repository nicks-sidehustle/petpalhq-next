## FAQ

### What if my dog gives up on puzzles?

Start easier — fill every compartment visibly. Gradually increase difficulty over 3–5 sessions.

### Can puppies use enrichment toys?

Yes — LickiMats and Kong Puppy from 8 weeks. Puzzles at 4–6 months.
